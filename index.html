CHAMOMILE TEA, LEMON JUICE & SALT

Materials:
1 roll of film (colour).
Film camera (any).
1 C of boiling water.
2 bags of chamomile tea.
2 Tbs of lemon juice.

Method:
Step 1 : Shoot your film using any film and film camera.
Step 2: Boil water and add the lemon juice and salt.
Step 3: Add film roll to the hot liquid emulsion and soak for 4 hours.
Step 4: Remove film roll and rinse with running cold water for 10 minutes.
Step 5: Leave the roll to dry indoors for at least 1 week.
Step 6: Develop your film the way you normally would.

HAND SOAP AND BOILING WATER

Materials:
1 roll of film (colour).
Film camera (any).
1 C of boiling water.
1 Tbs of hand soap.


Method:
Step 1 : Shoot your film using any film and film camera.
Step 2: Boil water and add the hand soap and mix.
Step 3: Remove the team bags and add the lemon juice to hot tea and mix.
Step 4: Add film roll to the hot liquid emulsion and soak for 4 hours.
Step 5: Stir the roll in the mixture for 1 minute every 20 minutes for the first 2 hours.
Step 6: Remove film roll and rinse with running cold water for 10 minutes.
Step 7: Leave the roll to dry indoors to dry for at least 1 week.
Step 8: Develop your film the way you normally would.

RED KOOLAID & DISHWASHING SOAP

Materials:
1 roll of film (colour).
Film camera (any).

Method:
Step 1 : Shoot your film using any film and film camera.

Step 5: Leave the roll to dry indoors for at least 1 week.
